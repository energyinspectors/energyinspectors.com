Services

HERS INSPECTIONS

Our field raters are not merely data takers--they are consultants. We understand the need of our client and assure that every possible challenge is met in meeting a client’s building deadline. We work with both superintendants and installing trades to ensure that your products are built with the best energy efficiency practices in mind.


Our certified HERS raters can all perform code, above-code, and solar inspections, streamlining your field process. They also work under central schedulers who keep our inspections on track with your needs. The HERS Rater is a vital element in our ability to provide our client with the best customer service and therefore is aware and takes to heart the importance of their tasks and is on top of every element of the inspections that are needed.

ENERGY MODELING

Our highly trained, dedicated team of compliance analysts will provide a timely, accurate assessment of your architectural drawings. With both residential and non-residential Certified Energy Plans Examiners, Certified Energy Analysts, LEED APs, and HERS Raters on staff, we will tailor your energy modeling solutions to the energy code and program requirements of your project. We provide multiple scenarios to assist in equipment selection and compliance. We have experience with multiple codes and multiple software programs, so we can cater to national builders and we understand the uniqueness of each builder and therefore tailor our models to match the builder's messaging and value.


Our energy modeling team will ensure that your plans meet any version of the T24 Code as well as any and all above-code programs you choose to participate in using the best and most cost-effective options available.
